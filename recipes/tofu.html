Vegan BBQ Teriyaki Tofu

This is a recipe for vegan BBQ teriyaki tofu. To make it vegan, honey is omitted, nevertheless, it is full of flavour.

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce: 4 tbsp
  • Soft brown sugar: 2 tbsp
  • Ground ginger: a pinch
  • Mirin: 2 tbsp
  • Sesame oil: 3 tsp
  • Very/extra firm tofu: 350g
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Mix the soy sauce, sugar, ginger, and mirin
  2. Drain the tofu block (e.g. by resting a weight (like a plate) on it for a while) and cut it into thick slices
  3. Marinade the slices in the mixture and chill for at least 1 hour
  4. Grill the slices on both sides for about 5 minutes, until they turn brown and go crisp on the edges
  5. Serve with sesame seeds scattered over (and possibly vegetables, such as broccoli
